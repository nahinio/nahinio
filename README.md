I’m a Computer Science undergrad who’s deeply curious about how things actually work under the hood. Most of my time goes into AI, especially theoretical machine learning and agentic systems, because I like breaking complex ideas down until they finally click.

At the same time, I try to build. Backend systems, a bit of blockchain, things that are meant to be reliable, not just impressive. I’m also trying to contribute to open source, slowly but consistently, learning by building alongside others. And when I’m not building, I try to solve problems through competitive programming, chasing that moment when a messy problem suddenly turns clean.

I’m working toward strong fundamentals, thoughtful systems, and the ability to handle problems that feel uncomfortable at first.
